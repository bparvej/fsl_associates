Our Practice Areas

Marriage and Divorce

Legal processes for marriage registration, dissolution of marriage (including talaq under Muslim law), and mutual consent divorce.

Child Custody and Guardianship

Determining custody arrangements post-divorce, parental rights, and guardianship matters.

Maintenance and Alimony

Legal obligations for spousal support and child maintenance, including enforcement of maintenance orders.

Adoption

Procedures and legal requirements for adopting a child, including consent and eligibility.

Inheritance and Succession

Issues related to the distribution of property and assets upon death, including intestate succession and wills.

Domestic Violence

Legal protections and remedies for victims of domestic abuse, including filing cases under specific laws.

Child Rights and Welfare

Advocating for the rights and welfare of children, including issues related to education, health, and social services.

Interfaith and Cross-Cultural Marriages

Legal considerations and challenges faced by couples from different religious or cultural backgrounds.

Legal Aid

Providing support to individuals who cannot afford legal representation in family matters.

Mediation and Alternative Dispute Resolution

Exploring amicable solutions through mediation before proceeding to court.
