I am facing issues regarding my ancestral land partition. My siblings are not agreeing to a mutual settlement. What are my legal options in Bangladesh?

Advocate M Alamgir

In cases where mutual settlement fails, you may file a Partition Suit in the relevant Assistant Judge Court. It is essential to have all land records (CS, SA, RS Porcha) and the deed of inheritance ready for the process.

Can a digital contract signed via email be enforced in a Bangladeshi court for a business dispute?

Advocate M Alamgir

Under the Information and Communication Technology Act, digital signatures and electronic records are admissible. However, specific evidentiary requirements must be met to prove the authenticity of the email trail.

What is the procedure for a check dishonor case under the NI Act?

Advocate M Alamgir

First, you must serve a legal notice within 30 days of the check dishonor. If payment isn't made within the notice period, you can file a case in the Magistrate Court within the following 30 days.

Is it mandatory to register a marriage if it was performed under Muslim law but abroad?

Advocate M Alamgir

Yes, for legal recognition in Bangladesh, particularly for inheritance or passport issues, it is highly recommended to register the marriage or obtain a certified Nikahnama through the relevant consulate or local authorities.
